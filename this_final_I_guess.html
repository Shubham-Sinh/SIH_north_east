Strategic Implementation Roadmap

Our vision is grounded in a phased, scalable plan to ensure sustainable impact and long-term adoption.

Phase 1: The Pilot

Launch MVP in 10 high-risk villages to validate models and gather feedback.

Timeline: 3 Months

Phase 2: The Scale-Up

District-wide rollout with full integration of IoT water sensors and advanced analytics.

Timeline: 9 Months

Phase 3: The Partnership

Partner with State Governments for wider, policy-level deployment and integration.

Timeline: 18 Months

Phase 4: The Vision

Integrate with the National Health Stack to contribute to a healthier India.
